The field of statistics can be divided into two broad areas namely descriptive statistics and inferential statistics. Descriptive statistics is that part of statistics involved with collection, presentation and description of numerical data. The term refers to the set of concepts and methods used in organizing, summarizing, tabulating, depicting and describing data. The data can be scores, ratings, ranks or indication of growing membership. The various types of descriptive statistics include measures of central tendency, measures of variability, tables, charts, graphs and measures of association.
